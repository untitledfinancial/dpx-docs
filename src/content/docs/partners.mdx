## What this is

We are inviting five treasury teams to join the **DPX Agent Treasury Pilot** — a private cohort for organizations that want to be at the frontier of autonomous treasury operations, not just observe it.

You are not testing software. You are helping define what programmable, agent-native institutional settlement looks like — and your use case will shape the protocol.

---

## Who this is for

You are a fit if any of these describes your team:

- **Treasury or payments engineering teams** at a fintech, bank, or asset manager currently evaluating programmable settlement infrastructure
- **AI/automation teams** building agent workflows that need a compliant, oracle-backed settlement primitive
- **ESG or sustainability leads** at institutions reporting under SFDR, CSRD, or FCA ESG rules who need a verifiable on-chain ESG audit trail
- **Innovation labs** at banks exploring stablecoin settlement, CBDC interoperability, or agent-native treasury — who need an external API-accessible settlement layer

You do **not** need to be at scale. You need to be serious about the problem.

## The cohort structure

**5 founding partners. Private. 90-day initial pilot.**

Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4): Sandbox integration — your team connects to the oracle APIs, runs fee quotes, and tests the agent settlement loop in sandbox mode. Zero on-chain commitment.

Phase 2 (Weeks 5–8): Live pilot — small-volume live settlements on Base mainnet with the full compliance layer. Real fees, real ESG redistribution, real on-chain audit trail.

Phase 3 (Weeks 9–12): Protocol feedback — structured sessions to capture what works, what needs changing, and what your organization needs to move to full production volume.

After 90 days: Founding Partners move to production on their own terms, with their permanent fee tier locked.

## Questions

**Is this free?** Sandbox mode is free. Live settlement fees apply at your locked partner rate. There is no cohort fee.

**Do I need to be on Base mainnet?** No. Sandbox mode requires no wallet. Live pilot requires a Base wallet with DPX tokens — we will help you set this up.

**What if we are not ready to settle live volume?** That is fine for Phase 1. We have partners who will start in sandbox and move to live once their internal integration is complete.

**Do you have a legal agreement?** Yes. A simple one-page mutual NDA and a partner letter are available on request before you commit to anything.
