## What DPX does

DPX is a programmable stablecoin settlement rail built for institutional treasuries and autonomous AI agents. Cross-border settlement in minutes, with fees 50–60% below correspondent banking. What makes it different: AI runs inside the oracles, not on the edges.

Every stability score, every ESG rating, every compliance signal is produced by an intelligence layer that reasons across 32+ real-world data sources simultaneously — market microstructure, macro indicators, geopolitical risk, capital flows, yield curves, climate data, ESG filings — and explains its conclusions in plain language. Treasury teams and risk managers get a synthesised briefing on every API call, not a raw number.

- **AI-powered Stability Oracle** — 7-tier signal pipeline spanning 32+ sources across market, macro, climate, and geopolitical data; every response includes plain-language reasoning, a confidence level, and a 30–90 day outlook
- **AI-powered ESG Oracle** — scores synthesised from 8 institutional sources (WorldBank, UN, OECD, IMF, SEC, and more); returns structured E/S/G breakdowns plus qualitative signals for treasury and ESG teams
- **Continuous compliance intelligence** — FATF, FCA, SEC, ECB, BIS, FSB, and OFAC monitored daily; an AI classifier identifies what's relevant to DPX; a persistent knowledge base grows over time
- **ESG-weighted fees** — high ESG score = lower fee; 100% of ESG fee redistributed to verified on-chain impact programs, with IPFS receipts
- **Predictable pricing** — fees enforced in the protocol, readable by any agent or TMS before execution
- **Non-custodial** — 7 contracts deployed on Base mainnet (chainId 8453), fully on-chain, agent-executable without human approval
