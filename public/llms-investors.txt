## ONE PARAGRAPH

DPX is the payment and settlement layer for the agentic internet — a compliance-grade
settlement rail where autonomous AI agents can discover, price, and execute cross-border
payments without human intervention. Any agent calls /manifest to discover DPX, /quote for
a binding fee, and POST /settle to execute via x402 USDC payment: no API key, no account,
no onboarding. The fee payment IS the authorization. Sender pays in USD, recipient receives
EUR — neither party touches crypto. Fees are 1.385% all-in versus 5.4%+ for Stripe or
2–5% for SWIFT. Seven contracts are live on Base mainnet, verified on Sourcify. The only
settlement rail with a native MCP server, x402 micropayment support, a 10-layer Stability
Oracle, and GENIUS Act (US) / MiCA (EU) compliance. Treasury management is the first
institutional vertical built on the agent layer. The comparable acquisition is
Bridge.xyz → Stripe at $1.1B (October 2024). DPX is post-build, pre-revenue, raising seed.
