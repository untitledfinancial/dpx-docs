# DPX — Investor Overview
# Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc.

> DPX is programmable, non-custodial settlement infrastructure for institutional
> treasuries and autonomous AI agents — where ethics is protocol, not just policy.

## Company

Untitled_ LuxPerpetua Technologies, Inc. operates at the intersection of
programmable finance, ESG accountability, and the emerging agentic economy.
DPX is the company's flagship protocol: a stablecoin settlement rail on Base
mainnet (Ethereum L2) that embeds ESG scoring, stability intelligence, and
fee transparency directly into settlement logic.

## Market

Cross-border institutional settlement is a $150T+ annual market dominated by
SWIFT and correspondent banking — infrastructure built in the 1970s, carrying
fees of 2–5% per transaction, with no machine-readable pricing, no ESG
accountability, and no path to autonomous execution.

DPX addresses this with a protocol-level alternative that is:
- Cheaper than incumbents (1.385% typical all-in vs 2–5% SWIFT)
- Machine-readable by design (any AI agent can discover, price, and execute)
- ESG-accountable by default (fee component tied to live ESG scores, 100%
  redistributed on-chain)
- Non-custodial (institutions keep custody; DPX is rails, not a bank)

## Product

### DPX Protocol
Deployed on Base mainnet (chainId 8453). Four-component fee structure enforced
on-chain via DPXSettlementRouter smart contract:
- Core transfer: 0.85%
- FX conversion: 0.40% (cross-currency only)
- ESG fee: (100 - esgScore) / 200 — live from oracle, 0–0.50%
- License: 0.01% fixed

Typical all-in rate (ESG score 75, cross-border): 1.385%
Best case (ESG score 100, same-currency): 0.86%

### Hybrid On-Chain / Off-Chain Architecture
Fee calculation happens off-chain via API (fast, readable by agents).
Fee enforcement happens on-chain (trustless, auditable).
/verify-fees endpoint confirms both match before any settlement executes.

### Agent-Native by Design
Any AI agent can go from discovery to executed settlement in five API calls
with no human in the loop. The protocol manifest returns
`onboarding.humanRequired: false`. Integrations ship for Claude (MCP),
ChatGPT (GPT Actions), LangChain, and n8n.

## Revenue Model

DPX earns fees on every settlement:
- Core + FX + License flow to protocol treasury
- ESG fee (0–0.50%) redistributes 100% to on-chain impact programs

Volume discount tiers (core fee reduction):
- Standard (<$100K/mo): 0% discount
- Growth ($100K–$1M/mo): 10% off core
- Institutional ($1M–$10M/mo): 20% off core
- Sovereign ($10M+/mo): 30% off core

At $5B annual volume (Sovereign tier), ESG pool alone: $6.25M/year
See full model: https://docs.untitledfinancial.com/financial-model
